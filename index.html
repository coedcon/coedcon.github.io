Secure Computation Industry Day


December 12, 2025

Bocconi University


On December 12th, we will be organizing a one-day invitation-only event focused on secure computation technolgies. Today, there are new technologies to protect sensitive data in untrusted environments such as secure multiparty computation (MPC), homomorphic encryption (FHE), and hardware-based confidential computing.

We plan to invite leading researchers and practitioners, creating excellent opportunities for networking and collaboration.

Space is limited, so we will prioritize attendance for representatives from companies that are most likely to benefit from the event.
