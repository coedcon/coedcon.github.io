Secure Computation Industry Day


December 12, 2025

Bocconi University


On December 12th, we are hosting a one-day, invitation-only event dedicated to the latest advances in secure computation technologies. These technologies enable organizations to analyze and collaborate on sensitive data without exposing it, even in untrusted environments.

The program will spotlight two breakthrough approaches:

  • Secure Multiparty Computation (MPC): allows multiple organizations to combine and process private data while ensuring that each partyâ€™s inputs remain confidential.
  • Fully Homomorphic Encryption (FHE): enables computation to be performed directly on encrypted data, so valuable insights can be extracted without ever revealing the underlying information.

Together, MPC and FHE are opening new opportunities for privacy-preserving data sharing, regulatory-compliant collaboration, and innovative digital services. From finance and healthcare to telecommunications and cloud computing, these technologies are redefining how industries can unlock value from data while keeping it secure.

We plan to invite leading researchers and practitioners, creating excellent opportunities for networking and collaboration.

Space is limited, so we will prioritize attendance for representatives from companies that are most likely to benefit from the event.
