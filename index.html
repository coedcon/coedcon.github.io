FHE Hardware Acceleration Summit

June 26, 2025

Stanford University



On June 26th, we will be organizing an one-day event focused on FHE. There are many approaches to accelerating FHE, targeting a range of platforms including CPUs, GPUs, FPGAs, and ASICs. We also observe a variety of techniques for accelerating low-level primitives such as NTT/FFT and more.

We plan to invite leading researchers and practitioners from both the hardware acceleration and application domains, creating excellent opportunities for networking and collaboration.

Space is limited, so we will prioritize attendance for representatives from companies that are most likely to benefit from the event.
